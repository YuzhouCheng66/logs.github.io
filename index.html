Abstract

Visual localization plays a crucial role in mobile robotics and augmented reality. Recent advances such as Gaussian Splatting have enabled realistic novel view synthesis, providing a promising foundation for localization tasks. However, existing methods typically require hundreds of training images to achieve good results. Method Overview

LoGS introduces an efficient pipeline for few-shot localization leveraging Gaussian Splatting. The system consists of two stages: map construction with limited training images, and robust online localization based on differentiable rendering and optimization. Details can be found in our paper.

Experiments

We demonstrate the performance of LoGS in challenging environments with few-shot training. The demo video shows qualitative results.
